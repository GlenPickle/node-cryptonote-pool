Android Mining

  1. Install the Pickaxe App from the Google Play Store
  2. Open the App and select "USE WALLET"
  3. At the coin selection screen choose Monero
  4. Click the settings option at the bottom of the app
  5. Open the "Pool" drop down menu and select "Custom Pool"
  6. Enter "xmr-mining-lottery.com:3333" as the Custom Pool URL and enter your Monero wallet address in the Username/Wallet field
  7. Choose your desired number of threads, click back to the miner tab of the app, and press the play button
If done properly, your hashrate will be displayed in the app
